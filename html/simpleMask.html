SimpleMask

v.0.1.1

A simple, small and easy to use JS string and input masker.

Stop making your site ashamed of your ugly masks.

Use mask!

P=Positive, D=Digit, L=Lowercase, U=Uppercase, C=Anycase, N=Numbers and Uppercase, n=Numbers and Lowercase, A=Alphanumeric
